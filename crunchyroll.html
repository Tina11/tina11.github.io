: Why I like Anime :

Because anime tells amazing stories! It’s romantic. It’s tragic. It’s funny. It’s dramatic. Anime has shown me a world of imaginations. When I’m watching a really good anime or reading a really good manga, the story has come to life, the characters have come to life. I wouldn't be able to put it down.

So far, One Piece remains close to the top because I’ve grown up with it. I’ve cried. I’ve laughed. I've been angry. These feelings make an anime very intimate. One Piece drags on sometimes but it continues to tell great stories of friendship, dreams, and perseverance. It is also the very first anime that has made me care so much about a boat that I felt so emotionally attached to it that it was just waterworks.

I’m so happy to see that anime is finally being more heavily adopted because no one would talk about anime before like we’re starting to now. It's wonderful that Crunchyroll is at the forefront of this movement. Thus, I was excited when I saw the posting for Sr. Product Designer at Crunchyroll because being a designer at a company where I can be my nerdiest would be a dream! I thought working here at Chewy was a dream because I love dogs but being a designer at Crunchyroll would take the dream job trophy! I've also included a link to my cover letter. My portfolio is on this site too. Thank you!
