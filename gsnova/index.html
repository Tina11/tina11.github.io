User Driven Strategy

During a collaborative kick-off meeting with us. You’ll help us understand everything there is to know about your business and your audience. Combining your extensive knowledge of your business, with our expertise in User Behavior, we’ll brainstorm with you right on the spot and come up with a solid UX strategy and architecture.

Strategy Driven Design

Before you leave our meeting, we’ve already started prototyping your site. With the knowledge we've gained about your audience and business goals, we’re able to implement our strategy into a tangible, fully designed prototype of the major site pages. Still in close collaboration with you, we’ll present and iterate on the fly.

Design Driven Development

The moment you’ve approved 50% of the designed prototype, we begin developing the back-end of the site while we wrap up design of the remaining pages with you. This allows us to get a head start on the core CMS functionality. The design and development teams work closely together to monitor version control, boosting productivity for launch.

How it Works

It's actually quite simple, working so closely with you, we’re able to make fast decisions and implement them even faster. We’re not skipping steps, we’re combing them to spend less time. Less time = less money.
